The Origin Story

rooted in the
soil of six states

Om Spices began with a single question: why does masala from the local shop taste nothing like what grandmother used? The answer led us down rural roads, into spice gardens, and across six states — building relationships with farmers who still grow the old way.

Three years, twelve farms, and one unwavering rule: if we wouldn't use it in our own kitchen, it doesn't leave the warehouse.
